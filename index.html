A simple rock-paper-scissor game

Rules

  • 1. Rock beats scissors
  • 2. Scissors beats Paper
  • 3. Paper beats Rock
  • Both players (player and computer in this case) choose one of the three options above. Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
    The winner of the round is determined by comparing the choices made by the participants
    If both participants choose the same option, it is a tie.
